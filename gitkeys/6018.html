Investments

 These are the investment products being offered to customers. 

 Use this sub-module to setup / create new investment products or edit existing investment products.

To access this window, click:Accounts Management - > Setup - > Financial Products - > Investments.

The following window will open up: 

Creating An Investment Product: 

Click on the ADD NEW  Button, to open the following screen:

On this Add screen, fill in the following fields: 

Name - Enter the name of the Investment Product

Minimum Balance - Enter the Minimum balance applicable for the Investment Product

Maximum Balance - Enter the maximum amount applicable for the Investment Product 

Recovery Priority -Enter the recovery priority of the investment product.  

An investment product that is given a recovery priority 1 means that it will be the first product to be recovered during loan repayment and so on. 

Maturity Period (Days) - Enter the period in days an investment product takes to mature. 

Annual Percentage Yield - Enter the annual percentage yield for the particular product.  

G/L Account - Click on the lookup icon to open the G/L Account Lookup dialog box: 

Select the appropriate account to be attached to the investment product. 

Parent Product  - Click on the lookup icon to open the Investment Product Lookup dialog box: 

Pool amount -Enter the pool Amount

Pool G/L Account - Click on lookup icon for the G/l pool account

Bill Reference number - Enter the bill reference number

Select the appropriate parent product  to be attached to the investment product being created.

Flags : 

The above check boxes represent actions to be associated with the investment product being created. Check accordingly.

Is Super Saver ? -  check if the product is to be a super saver. 

 Is Refundable ? -  Check to make the product refundable

 Is Pooled ? -  Check to enforce pooling actions on the product

Conceal MBanking Listing  ? -  check this to omit the product from product listing for certain operations e.g balance inquiry when user dials m banking ussd code.

Is Institutional Capital ?

 Is Locked ? -  Check to lock the product from being accessed and used. 

When done click on the CREATE Button to complete this task. 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.

Editing An Investment Product: 

Identify the the investment product to be edited: 

Then click on the edit icon against it to open the following window : 

On this Edit screen, modify the relevant fields - Name, Minimum Balance, Maximum Balance, Recovery Priority, Maturity Period (Days), Annual Percentage Yield, G/L  Account and Parent product. 

Click update to commit the changes into the system.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
