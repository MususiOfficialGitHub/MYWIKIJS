Branches

Use this sub module to capture a company's branch into the system.

Click on System Administration --> Navigate--> Setup--> Branches.

The following window will open up and the available branches are displayed. 

 Creating A Branch:

Click add to open the following window:

On the above screen:

Name - Type in the name of the branch being captured.

Company - Pick the company from the Company Lookup dialog box:

 Fill in the company's details - Address Lines 1 & 2, Street, Postal Code, City, E-mail, Land line & Mobile Line fields.

Click on the search icon to select Numbering Series.

Click create to commit the branch into the system.

On successful completion you will get a pop-up message confirming that the operation was successful.

  Refresh to view the new branch.
