Customer Accounts - Intra Account Transfers.

 Use this sub-module to  transfer money from customer accounts i.e from savings to repay loan or from investment to savings of same individual

To access this window, click :Accounts Module - > Operations - > Customers Account - > Intra - Account Transfer


 

The following screen will open up :

Intra Account Transfer Figure 1. 


 


 


 

Creating An  Intra- Account Transfer :

Click on Full Account Number look up icon to pick an origination account : 

Intra Account Transfer Figure 2.

 

   The associated account details will be displayed to the relevant fields as below : 


 

Intra Account Transfer Figure 3.


 


 

 Apportionment Tab :

Intra Account Transfer Figure 4.


 

Click on Full Account Number look up icon and select the recipient account from the dialog box :

Intra Account Transfer Figure 5.

 

       The additional account details will be populated to the respective fields: 


 

Intra Account Transfer Fig 6.


 


 

Amount - Enter amount to apportion to account.


 

Component:

 Intra-Account Transfer Fig 7


 

  • Aggregate - Choose Aggregate on Loan accounts for the system to distribute  Amount based on Standing Order 
  • Interest - Choose Interest for amount to go to Interest
  • Principle -  Choose Principle for amount to go to Principle.


 

Primary Description - Type in the appropriate primary description into this field. 

Secondary Description - Type in an appropriate Secondary description defining the transaction. 

                 Click on the add button to  commit the entry to the apportionment  tab just below: 

Intra Account Transfer Figure 8


 

   

Select an entry then click on the remove button to effect the removal of an erroneous entry.
