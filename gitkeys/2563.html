Cheques Transfer

This sub - module is used by the tellers to transfer cheques received during the day to the treasury.

To access this module, click on Front Office Management -> Operations -> Teller -> Cheque Transfer. 

The following window will open up:

The already received cheques will be displayed on the screen. 
NOTE: For details on how to receive a cheque, please refer to Cheque Deposit.

To see the cheques received you must have the rights and privileges of a teller.

Click on the refresh button to see the received cheques: 

Select the cheque(s) that you want to transfer by ticking in the check box against them. 

Use toggle selection to select all cheques at a go if that is what is required.

 

Click on the update button to transfer the cheques.

A proceed prompt will pop up.  Acknowledge and continue. 

 

On successful completion you will get a pop-up message confirming that the operation was successful.
