             Appraisal Recommendation .


 

                 This sub module is used by the appraiser to give recommendation about an employee's appraisal. 

The above steps will be carried out for each and every employee until all of the employees' appraisal recommendations are captured into the system. 

To access this window, click :Human Resources Module -> Navigate -> Operations -> Employees-> Performance Management ->Appraiser -> Recommendation. 


 

          The following screen will open up: 


 

Recommendation Fig 1


 

               On the above screen, fill in the following: 


 

 Employee - Click on the employee look up icon to open the following employee look up dialog box: 


 

Recommendation Fig 2


 

           From the above list, select the employee whose recommendation is to be captured. 

Appraisal Period - data will be automatically populated to this field, the active appraisal period will be selected in this case. 

Recommendation - Input the correct recommendation regarding the employee's appraisal findings into this field. 


 

                          Click create to save the information into the system. 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
