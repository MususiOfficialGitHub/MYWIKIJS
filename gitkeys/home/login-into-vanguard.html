Logging in / Logging out of Vanguard Financials

Open your browser and launch the application as shown in /home/getting-started 

First Time Logging In

To log into the system for the first time, your account must have been created by the Systems Administrator.

Once the Systems Administrator creates your account, you will receive a Text Alert (if you had subscribed) or Email (if you had subscribed) or both (that's if you had subscribed for both services). In both the Text and Email Alerts you will receive your USERNAME & PASSWORD.

Once you receive these credentials, use them to log into Vanguard Financials Application.
