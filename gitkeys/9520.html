Recurring Procedures

Use this sub-module to view recurring procedures batch that have been carried out in the system within a specific period of time.

The status of the entries normally defaults to posted.   

To access this window, click: Customer Relationship Management - > Operations - > Utilities - > Recurring Procedures

The following screen will open up:

All the posted entries will be displayed on the screen. 

The other statuses of the entries can also be viewed here. 

Select the appropriate grouping from the drop-down menu:

Refresh to reload data onto the screen.
