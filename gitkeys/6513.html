Inter Account Transfer Batch

               This module is used to transfer money  from savings to repay loan ,savings to boost shares or shares to increase savings.

                The transfers mainly happens among different accounts( different customer accounts/ customer to g/l accounts). 

           It can also transfer money to G/L accounts against customer accounts and vice versa but for the same customer using a single customer account.


 

To access this window, click :Accounts Management -> Navigate - > Operations - > Batch Origination- > Inter-Account Transfer


 

The following screen will open up:

Inter-Account Transfer Origination Batch Fig 1.
