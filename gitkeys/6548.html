      Mobile To Bank  Verification


 

             Use this sub-module to verify mobile to bank transactions that have been reconciled.

To access this window, click: Accounts Management - > Operations - >  Mobile To Bank - > Verification.


 

          The following screen will open up : 

Mobile To Bank Verification Fig 1


 

    Identify the mobile to bank request to be verified, click on the verification icon against it to open the following screen : 


 

Mobile To Bank Verification Fig 2

   Provide appropriate remarks, select the action then update.
