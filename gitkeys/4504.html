Directors

This sub - module is used  for creating Directors.

To access this module, click on Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Operations =>> Members Management =>> Directors

The following window will open up:

Creating A Director

Click on the Add New, to open the following screen:

Go ahead and proceed to select the director from the customer lookup to open the following Customer Lookup dialog box: 

Search for and select the customer.

Remarks -  Enter remarks into this field accordingly. 

 Click on the CREATE Button to save the director into the system. 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.

Editing A Director

To edit a Director's information: 

Identify the director to be edited, right click on it and click edit ;

against him to open the following window:

Make necessary changes to the director's details.

Check the  

option  to lock a director. 

When a director is locked, he/she will not be able to enjoy the benefits of being a director in the system.

 Click on the UPDATE Button to save the changes into the system.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
