Credit Types

Use this sub-module to set up the various Credit Types for example; salary, dividends, casual workers pay etc.

In this sub-module you can also tag applicable charges to that Credit Type, attach loan products, and also attach investment products that will be applicable to that credit type.

To access this window, click:Accounts Management - > Setup - >Miscellaneous -> Credit Types

The following screen will open up: 

  Creating  A Credit Type :

Click on the ADD NEW  Button, to open the following screen:

On the above screen, fill in the required fields.

Name - Enter the name of the Credit Type to be created  

G/L Account - Click on the lookup icon to select the appropriate G/L Account for that Credit Type: 

Revenue tax :click on the lookup to select the appropriate Revenue Tax

 Payroll number types click on the lookup to select

Unallocated funds Destination -  select from the drop down the appropriate  destination  whether investment or savings.

Unallocated funds Product :click on the lookup and select the appropriate Product.

Transaction Ownership - Select the appropriate type of transaction ownership from the drop down list: 

Applicable Charges -  select by clicking on all the applicable charges: 

 For example if it's a salary processing Credit Type, the applicable charges can be "salary processing commission" 

 Applicable direct debits - select  the applicable direct debits. 

NOTE : The applicable direct debits are the debits previously created in the direct debits sub module. 

 To capture a new / applicable one, please refer to Direct Debits. 

 Offsetable Loan Products - Select Loan products that are to be tagged to this Credit Type: 

Toggle selection is  to select all either Bosa products or Fosa Products

     Concession-Exempt Loan Products - select the concession exempt loan products  that are to be attached to this credit type.

Concession exempt investment products -  select the concession exempt investment products  that are to be attached to this credit type.

  


 

Offsettable Savings Products - select the savings products  that are to be attached to this credit type:

                   When done click on the CREATE Button to complete this task.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.

                                         

Flags

Suppress account Alerts?- Check the flag to suppress account alert

Suppress minimum Balance validation? -check to suppress minimum balance validation

Suppress grace period validation? - check to suppress grace period validation

Enable hybrid loan recovery? - Check to enable hybrid loan recovery

Apportion unallocated funds? -check it to apportion unallocated funds

Recover indefinite charges? -Check the flag to recover indefinite charges

Recover carry forwards? - check the flag to recover carry forwards

Recover Sundry arrearages? -check the flag to recover sundry arrearages

Suppress multi-Payout Recovery? - check the flag to suppress multi-payout recovery

Tax inclusive? -Check it to make the credit type tax inclusive

Validate interest capitalization? - check the flag to validate interest capitalization

Execute adhoc standing orders? -check the flag to execute adhoc standing orders

Throttle scoring? -check the flag to allow throttle scoring

Validate indefinite charges? -check the flag to validate indefinite charges

Is locked ?- check it to make  the credit type no longer operational


 

       Editing A Credit Type: 


 

 First Identify the Credit Type to be edited : 

Credit Types Fig 10.


 

     Then click on the edit icon against it to open the following screen


 

Credit Types Fig 11.


 

        On this Edit screen, modify the data on the necessary fields - Name, G/L Account ,Transaction Ownership,  Applicable Charges, Applicable direct debits, Attached Loan Products, Concession-Exempt Loan Products, Attached Investment Products &  Attached Savings Products.

                        When done click on the UPDATE Button to complete this task.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
