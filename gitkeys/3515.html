         Take Exit Interview


 

        Use this sub-module to carry out employee exit interview on the exiting employee.  

To access this window, click : Human Resources Module -> Navigate -> Operations  - > Employees -> Exit -> Exit Interview -> Take Interview. 


 

The following screen will open up:        

                                                                                                                                       

Take Interview Fig 1


 

     On the above screen:

Employee - This field will be automatically populated with the full names of the employee who is logged in. 


 

Exit Interview Questions Pane: 

Take Interview Fig 2


 

Question - This field will be pre - populated with interview questions created in the set up section. 

Answer -   Type in the answer to the question in the text field provided. 

      To answer the next question, click on the next button on the numbering bar and the question will be displayed on the question field.  

      See below : 

     


 

              Click on the save button 

to save the information into the system. 


 

                       

REMEMBER:  You'll have to click on the save button for every question answered. 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
