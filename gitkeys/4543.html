FingerPrint

    Use this sub-module to capture a member's fingerprint information into the system.

To access this window, click :Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Operations =>> Biometric Register =>> Fingerprint


 

The following window will open up: 


 

FingerPrint Fig 1


 


 

 Creating A Customer In The Biometric Register:


 

          Click on the create button to open up the following screen: 

FingerPrint Fig 2


 

Customer Name - Look up and select the member whose biometric information is to be captured.

                            The remaining customer details will be automatically populated.

Preferred Finger Digit -  Select the member's preferred finger digit from the drop down list : 

                                       

Provide appropriate remarks in the remarks field. 

You can check 

flag to enforce withdrawals using fingerprints authentication

   Click on the create to add the member to the bio-metric register.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.


 

                                 


 


 

       Editing A Customer On The Biometric Register:


 

 Identify the person to be modified then click on the edit icon 

  to open the following window:


 

FingerPrint Fig 3

  

      On the above window, changes can only be made to the customer's preferred finger digit and remarks. 

              Click on the update button to save the changes into the system.  


 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
