Employment Sectors

Use this sub module to capture an employer's zones into the system.

To access this sub module click on: Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Setup =>> Employment Sectors
The following window will open up : 

 All the available sectors are displayed on the screen.

Creating An Employment Sector:

Click on the add button to open the following screen :

On the above screen :       

Define an appropriate Name and Median income for the sector being captured then create

Is locked flag-Check is locked to render employment sector locked and not operational

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.

 

Editing An Employment Sector:

 Identify the sector to be edited then right click on the entry to see the edit button as shown below:

View Audit log icon -click to view the specific audit log of the entry selected

Click on the edit icon against the sector you need to make changes to open the following window:

Modify all the fields appropriately.

Click update to commit the changes into the system.

Is locked flag-Check is locked to render employment sector locked and not operational

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
