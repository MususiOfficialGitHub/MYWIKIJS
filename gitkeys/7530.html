Movement Catalogue. 


 

The inventory movement Catalogue sub - module is used to monitor the transfer of inventory from one department to another within the institution. 


 

To access this sub module click on: Procurement and Assets Management -> Navigate -> Operations -> Inventory->  Movement Catalogue. 


 

The following screen will open up :

                                                                                                                                               

Asset Movement Fig 1


 

       All the asset movements are displayed. 


 


 

Refresh to view the new transfers.
