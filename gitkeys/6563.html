 Supplements

 

                   Use this sub-module to carry out the Supplements of the institution's  Budget.

To access this window, click :Accounts Management - > Operations- > Budget Management - > Supplements

The following window will open.


 

Create a budget supplement

Fill in the necessary details lookup the source budget and the destination budget respectively

Enter the Description and amount effectively

Select the adjustment type either addition or deduction

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
