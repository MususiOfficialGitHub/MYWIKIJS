Appraisal Summary


 

              This sub module will be used by the appraiser / management to view the summary of the employees appraisals. 

To access this window, click :Human Resources Module -> Navigate -> Operations -> Employees-> Performance Management ->Appraiser -> Appraisal Summary. 


 

          The following screen will open up : 


 

Appraisal Summary Fig 1

   

        To view an employee(s) appraisal summary, on the above screen, do the following: 


 

Appraisal Period -  Click on the period look up icon to open the following Employee Appraisal Period Look Up dialog box: 


 

Appraisal Summary Fig 2


 

           Pick the correct appraisal period. 

     The period's Start Date & End Dates   will be automatically populated to their respective fields.


 

      Employee Details: 


 

Appraisal Summary Fig 3


 

       Employee -  Click on the employee look up dialog box to open the following employee look up dialog box: 


 

Appraisal Summary Fig 3


 

         From the above list, search for and select the correct employee whose appraisal summary is to be viewed.  


 

  The employee's Department &  Branch  data will be populated to the respective fields.


 

     

      Appraisal Summary Pane: 


 

   On the appraisal summary pane, data will be automatically populated to the fields once the customer is selected.
