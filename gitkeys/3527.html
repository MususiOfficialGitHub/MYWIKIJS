Salary Heads

        Salary heads are components that constitute Pay Structures. 

         A salary head could be an earning, which is paid to an employee, or a deduction, which is recovered from an employee's salary. 

       The value of these Pay Heads could either be fixed or variable, for each Payroll period.

To access this module click on: Human Resource Module ->Navigate ->Operations ->Salary ->Salary Heads


 

                          The following screen opens up : 


 

Salary Heads Fig 1


 

            All available salary heads are displayed. 


 

Creating A Salary Head :


 

Click on the create button, the following screen will open up :


 

Salary Heads Fig 2


 

         On the above screen :


 

          Type - Select the salary head type from the drop down menu :


 

Salary Heads Fig 3


 

  Product -  Link the salary head to a product from the Product Lookup dialog box :

                   

NOTE: The product to be selected is informed by the salary head type selected.

                                      Basic pay, NHIF,NSSF,PAYE, Provident Fund deductions  & Other earning are tied to savings product. 

                                      Investment & Loan Deductions are tied to Investment & Loan products respectively.

                              

Salary Heads Fig 4


 

              Name - Provide an appropriate salary head name.


 

G/L Account -  Link the Salary Head to its representation in the GL chart of accounts by clicking on the G/L Account look up icon to open the following G/L Account Look Up dialog box.

 Select the correct G/L Account to be linked to the salary head: 


 

Salary Heads Fig 5


 

                           Click create to commit the salary head to the system.  

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.


 

                           


 


 

                                        Refresh to view the newly created salary head.

    Identify the salary head to be edited.

               Click on the edit icon 

against the salary head to open the following screen :


 

Salary Heads Fig 6


 

          Make the necessary changes to the entries to be affected.
