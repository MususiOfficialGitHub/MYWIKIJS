Membership Activation

Use this sub-module to activate a rejoining member's accounts

NOTE :  This is only applicable for members who had partially terminated and are now rejoining

To access this sub module Click on;

Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Operations =>> Members Management =>> Membership Activation

The following window opens up : 

Activating A Member:

Select the member to be activated from the search icon details will be populated.

Verify the information then update to effect the activation

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
