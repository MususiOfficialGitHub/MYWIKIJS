Customer Queries.

     Use this sub-module to confirm an existent customer's details against the government's Integrated Population Registration System (IPRS) system.

To access this window, click :

Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Operations =>> Population Register Queries =>> Customer Queries


 

The following window will open up: 

Customer Queries Fig 1

        Use this sub module to make requests for customers that already exist in the master register. 


 


 

             To originate a customer query, select create to open the following window : 

Customer Queries Fig 2

  On the above screen:

Click on the lookup icon to search for the customer. 

Make use of the available search options to find then select the member. 

Details will be populated as below : 

Customer Queries Fig 3

  Provide appropriate remarks in the remarks field then create. 


 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.


 

                                     


 

 The request will be captured as new: 

Customer Queries Fig 4.

    Once approved, the request will be made against the government's IPRS database and the results will be pulled and displayed under the Queried status.
