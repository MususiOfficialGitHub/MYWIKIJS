Refund Notification Verification

        Use this sub-module to verify a Refund request.

To access this window, click :Credit Management - > Operations - > Refund Processing ->  Verification 


 

The following screen opens up :


 

Refund Notification Verification Fig 1.

 

    Click on the look up icon and select a previously approved request : 


 


 

Details will show as below :

Refund Notification Verification Fig 2.

            Cross check and ensure the entry details are correct. 

Remarks - Provide Audit remarks in the remarks field. 

        Pick the Verify/Defer option from the drop down menu.

        Click on the update Button to effect the verification. 

    

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
