Training Facilitators

Use this sub-module to capture training facilitators.

To access this window, click: Human Resources Module -> Navigate -> Setup - > Training Facilitators

 The following window will open up:

Available entries are displayed. 

Creating A New Facilitator 

Click on the Add new button and the following screen will open up:        

  

Name -  Provide the facilitator's details then create

On successful completion you will get a pop-up message confirming that the operation was successful.

 

Editing A facilitator: 

 Identify and right-click the entry to be modified.

Then click on the edit icon against it to open the following window:

Make applicable changes.

 Check the is locked if it's no longer used.

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
