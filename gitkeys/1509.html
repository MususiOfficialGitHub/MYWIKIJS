Default

Use this sub module define workflow chain for the available origination types. 

To access this Module, click on System Administration - > Operations - > Workflow - > Default.

The following screen opens up: 

Updating Workflow:

Type - Select a Permission type from the drop down as shown below.

Roles - in the roles tab, choose a role and specify number or approvers. 

Priority -   Priority updates automatically on your type of selection.

Settings  

  • Require biometrics? - check to make use of biometric features.
  • Bypass Role-Based Approval levels? - check to bypass   Role-based approval levels.
  • Enforce Role-based Approval After Department Levels? - check to enforce Role-based approval After Department Levels.

Click Update to commit the changes into the system

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
