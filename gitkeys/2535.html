Liquidation

Use this sub module to make payments of matured Fixed Deposits.

To Access this sub module, Click on Front-Office Management -> Operations -> Fixed Deposits -> Liquidation. 

The following screen will open up:

NOTE:  You can only pay fixed deposits that are matured. 
Call Deposits can be paid out too, since they are accessible any time.  

Use the search filters available or use the date ranges to search for the fixed deposit to be liquidated, then click on the REFRESH Button to reload data onto the screen.

Right click on the account that you want to pay and click on the Liquidate option.

On clicking, the following page opens:

 

To finalize the Task, click on the UPDATE Button.

On successful completion you will get a pop-up message confirming that the operation was successful.
