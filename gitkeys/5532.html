 Loan Interest Charging (Ad hoc)

           Use this sub-module to charge  Interest  on the Loan Products (Addition of unpaid interest on a loan to its principal balance) at a member's will.

To access this window, click :Credit Management- > Operations - > Recurring Procedures - > Loan Interest  Charging(Ad hoc). 


 

The following screen will open up :

Loan Interest Charging (Ad hoc) Fig 1.


 

        On the above screen : 


 

Customer Name  -Click on the lookup icon to open the Customer Lookup dialog box


 

      Loan Interest Charging (Ad hoc) Fig 2


 

    Search for and select the customer whose interest is to be charged. 

           The selected customer's details - employer, station, account number, member number and personal file number - will be populated to the respective fields.

                

NOTE: Depending on the selected customer, will display the screen below
