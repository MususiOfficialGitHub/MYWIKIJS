Loan Products Exemptions

Use this sub-module to exempt customers from a particular loan products.

To access this sub module Click on;

Registry Management =>> Navigate =>> Operations =>> Members Management =>> Loan Products Exemptions

The following window opens up : 

Adding A Member to Loan product exemption list:

Click on the Edit icon by right clicking on the selected entry to be edited as shown

Against the loan product you intend to exempt a customer

Details will be populated as below:

Use the search icon to select customers

Loan Products Exemptions

Selecting the customer will populate the rest of the fields.

Remarks - Fill in the remarks field and then click on the Add button to add the selected customer to the list of the selected Loan product.

On the Exempted customers pane, the list of all members exempted from the loan product selected are displayed. To Remove a member from the list,   make selection as below 

then click the Remove button to discard the member(s) from the list. 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
