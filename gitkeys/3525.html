Leave Approval


 

           Use this sub module to approve the employee leave requests. 

To access this module, click on: Human Resources Module -> Navigate ->Operations-> Leave ->Approval. 


 

           The following window will open up :


 


 

Leave Approval Fig 1. 


 

             The pending requests will be displayed on the screen. 


 

         Leave Approval :

NOTE: The leave approval process has been implemented in a workflow. The entries are only visible to users under roles within the approval chain

      

              Click on the approval icon 

against the application to open the following window :


 

Leave Approval Fig 3. 


 

        Remarks - Provide appropriate approval remarks in the remarks field. 

                Pick the Approve option from the drop down menu. 

                              Update to approve the leave application.


 

On successful completion you will get a pop up message confirming that the operation was successful.
