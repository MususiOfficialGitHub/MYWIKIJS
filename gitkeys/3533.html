Salary Period Closing.

         This is the final stage of salary processing. 

         Use this sub module to close the Salary Period; which will in turn post the salaries into the employee accounts.

 

NOTE:  Once a salary period has been closed it cannot be opened or amended.

To access this module, click on Human resource -> Navigate -> Operations -> Salary -> Period Closing.
